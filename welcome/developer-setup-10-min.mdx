---
title: "Developer Setup (10 min)"
---

This is the fastest path to start building for MARS.

## Goal

Get an editor connected to your robot over SSH in about 10 minutes.

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install a VSCode-based editor">
    Use VSCode, Cursor, Windsurf, or another compatible VSCode fork.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Remote SSH support">
    Install the `Remote - SSH` extension if it is not already built in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your robot IP">
    In the app, open **Configuration -> WiFi** and copy the robot IP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect over SSH">
    Run `ssh jetson1@<robot-ip>` from your terminal, or connect through your IDE.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your robot workspace">
    Open your project folder on the robot, for example `~/innate-os`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
