## What is available now

Manipulation skills powered by end-to-end models show up in the app under **Skills** -> **Physical Skills** once they are available on your robot.

At this stage, these learned skills are **mobile manipulation skills**: they combine movement in the environment with arm actions, rather than arm-only stationary routines.
